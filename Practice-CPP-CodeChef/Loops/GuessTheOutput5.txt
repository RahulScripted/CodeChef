What does the following code snippet do?


#include <iostream>
using namespace std;
int main() {
    for (int i = 1; i <= 10; i++) {
        cout << i * 2 << " ";
    }
}



Select one of the following options:

- Prints even numbers from 1 to 10
- Prints numbers from 1 to 10
- Prints multiples of 2 from 2 to 20
- Prints numbers from 2 to 20



Correct Answer: Prints multiples of 2 from 2 to 20

Explanation: The loop iterates from 1 to 10, and the statement cout << i * 2 << " "; prints multiples of 2
