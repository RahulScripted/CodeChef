Chef and Chefina are playing with dice. In one turn, both of them roll their dice at once. They consider a turn to be good if the sum of the numbers on their dice is greater than 6. Given that in a particular turn Chef and Chefina got X and Y on their respective dice, select the options which are good turns.


Select all the correct options.

1. X = 3, Y = 4
2. X = 3, Y = 3
3. X = 4, Y = 4
4. X = 3, Y = 2



Correct Answer(s):
1. X = 3, Y = 4
3. X = 4, Y = 4
